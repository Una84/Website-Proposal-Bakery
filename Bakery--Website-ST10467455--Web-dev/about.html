Our Story

Founded in 2020, Sweet Crust is a family-owned bakery in Cape Town known for our artisan breads, flaky pastries, and beautiful custom cakes.

Mission: To deliver freshly baked, high-quality goods that bring joy to the community.

Vision: To be Cape Town’s leading artisanal bakery, recognized for quality, innovation, and connection.
