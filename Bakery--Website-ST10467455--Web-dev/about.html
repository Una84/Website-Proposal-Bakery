Our Story

Sweet Crust began in 2020 as a small family bakery in Cape Town. Our mission is to bring people together through delicious, handmade baked goods made from locally sourced ingredients.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Deliver freshly baked, high-quality goods that bring joy to the community.

Vision: Be Cape Town’s leading artisan bakery, known for quality, innovation and community.
